**Welcome to the FIFA World Cup Database Project**

**The Beautiful Game, in Data**

In this project, we're taking the excitement of the FIFA World Cup and turning it into a sleek, data-driven experience. With a comprehensive database at its core, we're giving fans a deeper dive into the beautiful game, exploring the triumphs and heartbreaks of the world's top teams.

**The Database: A Masterpiece of Code**

Our database, "world_cup," is a masterpiece of code, carefully crafted to store and analyze the data of the world's most prestigious sporting event. With a table for games, we're able to track every kick, every goal, and every triumph since 2014. From the Round of 16 to the final whistle, our database is the ultimate source for World Cup enthusiasts.

**The Games Table: A Storyteller's Dream**

Within our database, the "games" table is where the magic happens. With columns for year, round, winner, opponent, winner goals, and opponent goals, we're able to reconstruct the thrilling narrative of each match. Whether it's a dramatic comeback or a convincing win, our database has the details.
